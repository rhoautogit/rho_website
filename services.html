The journey

Most teams don't fail at policy. They fail when policy has to translate into data, controls, and production systems.

Step 01
Get Bank-Ready
The challenge
You are preparing for a sponsor Bank relationship or building your compliance foundation for the first time.
What we do
AML/KYC/KYB program design, policies and procedures, risk assessments, and sponsor Bank readiness. We build what your Bank partner will expect to see on day one.
A compliance program that gets approved, not sent back.
Step 02
Make It Audit-Proof
The challenge
Your program exists, but now the Bank, auditor, or regulator wants proof that it actually works.
What we do
Data lineage mapping, audit trail validation, control evidence, model validation for AI/ML and rules-based tools, and BCBS 239-style data integrity frameworks. We connect your compliance program to the data that proves it.
You can show how your data works, not just say it does.
Step 03
Make It Work in Production
The challenge
You have selected a vendor or platform, but implementation is stuck because compliance, product, and engineering are not aligned.
What we do
Partner with vendors and internal teams to translate regulatory requirements into implementation logic, technical requirements, control documentation, and go-live readiness.
Vendor implementations that work, scale, and stand up to scrutiny.
